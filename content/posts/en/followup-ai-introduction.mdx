# FollowPack: Turn Meeting Notes into Professional Follow-ups in Seconds

> Say goodbye to tedious manual organization. Let AI do all the work from meeting notes to complete follow-up packages.

---

## 🎯 Product Overview

**FollowPack** is an AI-powered meeting follow-up tool that transforms raw meeting notes into complete follow-up packages including follow-up emails, action items, meeting summaries, and CRM notes - all in just a few seconds.

### Core Features

- **🚀 Lightning Fast**: From pasting notes to getting complete follow-up packages in just 10 seconds
- **📝 Professional Templates**: AI prompts optimized for 4 common meeting types
- **🎨 Flexible Tones**: 3 tone styles with one-click switching
- **🔒 Privacy First**: Anonymous mode, no registration required
- **☁️ Cloud Storage**: Meeting notes auto-saved, view history anytime

## 🎨 Features

### 1. Smart Input

Multiple input methods supported:

- **Direct Paste**: Paste meeting notes text, minimum 30 characters
- **File Upload**: Support .txt, .md text files
- **Audio Support**: Future versions will support .mp3, .m4a, .wav formats

### 2. Professional Templates

Optimized generation templates for different meeting types:

| Meeting Type | Use Case | Output Features |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Sales Discovery Call** | First contact, qualification screening | Highlights needs, budget, timeline |
| **Requirements Discussion** | Deep dive into scope, constraints, success criteria | Detailed feature requirements and priorities |
| **Project Kickoff** | Goal alignment, owners, milestones | Clear task assignments and timeline |
| **Weekly Client Update** | Status updates, blockers, action items | Concise progress reporting |

### 3. Tone Styles

3 tone styles for different scenarios:

| Tone | Characteristics | Best For |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| **Professional** | Formal, rigorous | First-time collaboration, important clients |
| **Friendly** | Warm yet professional | Long-term collaboration, familiar clients |
| **Concise** | Direct, no fluff | Quick decisions, internal communication |

### 4. Complete Output

One generation, get complete follow-up materials:

- 📧 **Follow-up Email** - Professional email subject, structured body, ready to send
- 📋 **Action Items** - Clear task descriptions, assigned owners, due dates
- 📄 **Meeting Summary** - 4-7 sentence concise summary covering goals, needs, decisions, next steps
- 💼 **CRM Notes** - Notion/HubSpot style, key facts in bullet points, easy to import to CRM
