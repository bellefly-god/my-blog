# Introducing My Tools: Page Cleaner & Notion Template Manager

As an indie developer, I love building tools that solve real problems. Today, I'm excited to introduce two tools I've been working on.

---

## Page Cleaner 🧹

**The Problem:** Modern websites are cluttered. Ads, sidebars, popups, and endless distractions make it hard to focus on what actually matters—the content.

**The Solution:** Page Cleaner is a Chrome extension that strips away all the noise with a single click.

### Features

- **One-Click Cleaning** - Instantly remove ads, sidebars, and distractions
- **AI-Powered Analysis** - Get summaries and key points from any article
- **Clean Reading Mode** - Focus on content without the clutter
- **Side Panel Integration** - Clean view opens in Chrome's side panel

### How It Works

1. Install from Chrome Web Store
2. Navigate to any webpage
3. Click the extension icon
4. Enjoy a clean, distraction-free reading experience

### Perfect For

- 📚 Research and reading articles
- 📝 Content curation
- 🎯 Focused work sessions
- 📱 Mobile-friendly content consumption

---

## Notion Template Manager 📋

**The Problem:** Notion is powerful, but setting up new pages from scratch is time-consuming. And finding that perfect template you used last month? Good luck.

**The Solution:** Notion Template Manager helps you organize, discover, and apply templates instantly.

### Features

- **Template Library** - Curated collection of beautiful templates
- **Quick Duplication** - One click to copy any template to your workspace
- **Smart Organization** - Categorize and search your templates
- **Cross-Workspace Sync** - Use your templates across multiple Notion workspaces

### How It Works

1. Connect your Notion workspace
2. Browse the template library
3. Click to duplicate any template
4. Customize and make it yours

### Perfect For

- 🏢 Project management setups
- 📊 Dashboard creation
- 📝 Note-taking systems
- 🎯 Goal tracking

---

## Why I Built These

After years of working in tech, I noticed two things:

1. **Information overload is real** - We're bombarded with content daily. Tools that help us focus are invaluable.

2. **Repetitive setup kills productivity** - Every time you start a new project, you waste time on setup. Templates solve this.

Both tools address these pain points in different ways. Page Cleaner helps you consume content better. Notion Template Manager helps you create content faster.
