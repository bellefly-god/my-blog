# Welcome to Inkwell

Thank you for visiting Inkwell. This is a minimal blog designed with one purpose: to let your words shine.

## Why Inkwell?

In a world of complex content management systems, sometimes you just need a simple place to write. Inkwell offers:

- **Clean design** - Minimal distractions, maximum focus on your content
- **Elegant typography** - Serif headlines paired with a readable sans-serif body
- **Dark mode** - Automatic theme detection with manual toggle
- **MDX support** - Write in Markdown with the power of React components
- **Fast performance** - Built on Next.js 14 for optimal speed

## Getting Started

Adding new posts is simple. Just create a new `.mdx` file in the `content/posts` directory with frontmatter for the title, date, and excerpt.

```mdx
---
title: "Your Post Title"
date: "2024-03-14"
excerpt: "A brief description of your post."
---

Your content here...
```

Good writing deserves a good home. Inkwell provides that home - unobtrusive, elegant, and focused on what matters: your words.
